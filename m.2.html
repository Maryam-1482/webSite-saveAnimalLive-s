Efforts to keep species from extinction

Some animals are at risk of extinction due to their small numbers or because of their high predation or change in their environment, and protecting these animals is ensuring the preservation of wildlife for future generations, and given the importance of this issue, the environment and its impact on the sustainability of wildlife in the local environment There are many countries that do a lot of effort to preserve and protect these animals, and the most famous of these countries are:

  1. Gulf countries.
  2. The United Arab Emirates.
  3. The United States.

Some of these efforts are:

  1. Establishing many nature reserves
  2. Establishing policies and systems to protect the environment, which leads to a decrease in the extinction rate of some animals
  3. Environmental awareness and education
  4. Establishing specialized groups to protect animals
  5. Establishment of World Animal Day
